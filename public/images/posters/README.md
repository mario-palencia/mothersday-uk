# Poster Images - Nighttime Skylines

## Overview

These images are used as poster/placeholder images for the hero video section. They display while the video is loading, providing an immediate visual context of the city.

## Image Specifications

### Format
- **Preferred:** JPG (optimized) or WebP (for better compression)
- **Format:** Landscape orientation
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or similar (matches video format)

### Technical Requirements
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **File Size:** Optimized for web (< 500KB recommended)
- **Color Profile:** sRGB
- **Quality:** High quality but optimized for web

### Content Requirements
- **Theme:** Nighttime skyline of the city
- **Style:** Romantic, atmospheric, suitable for Valentine's Day
- **Recognition:** Must be clearly recognizable as the specific city
- **Mood:** Should convey romance and urban beauty

## File Naming Convention

```
skyline-[city-slug].jpg
```

### Examples
- `skyline-madrid.jpg` - Nighttime skyline of Madrid
- `skyline-barcelona.jpg` - Nighttime skyline of Barcelona
- `skyline-new-york.jpg` - Nighttime skyline of New York
- `skyline-paris.jpg` - Nighttime skyline of Paris
- `skyline-london.jpg` - Nighttime skyline of London

## How to Add Images

1. **Source the Image:**
   - Find or create a high-quality nighttime skyline photo of the city
   - Ensure it's recognizable and romantic/atmospheric

2. **Optimize the Image:**
   - Resize to at least 1920x1080
   - Compress for web (use tools like TinyPNG, ImageOptim, or Squoosh)
   - Save as JPG or WebP

3. **Name the File:**
   - Use the format: `skyline-[city-slug].jpg`
   - Example: `skyline-madrid.jpg`

4. **Place in Directory:**
   - Save to: `/public/images/posters/`

5. **Test:**
   - The image will automatically appear as the video poster
   - It shows while the video loads and if the video fails to load

## Tips

- **Consistency:** Try to maintain a similar style/atmosphere across all city images
- **Recognition:** Include iconic landmarks or distinctive city features
- **Romance:** Choose images that convey a romantic, intimate atmosphere
- **Nighttime:** Ensure images are clearly nighttime scenes (city lights, illuminated buildings)
- **Quality:** Prioritize sharp, clear images over artistic blur effects
